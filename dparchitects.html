Design Partnership Architects

I worked for Angelo Petrozzelli at Design Partnership Architects over the course of more than three years while I was in high school and during my pre-college gap year. I assisted the architect, Angelo Petrozzelli with a variety of tasks. I was responsible for producing computer drawn floor plans and elevations for the jobs we were working on. I also assisted with clerical tasks like preparing client contracts and meeting notes. Furthermore, I would participate in site visits to measure and document new projects as well as acting as an assistant/observer for client meetings.

The building on the left is St. Patricks church in Natick, MA. The firm has done many projects for this church the most recent of which was to add a new entryway and vestibule onto one side of the building. The building on the right is Snowdrop Montessori school in Haverhill, MA. They were moving into a new building and we created plans to update the bare space per their requirements.
