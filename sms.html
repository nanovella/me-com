Soil Moisture Sensors

This project began as an independent study last year at Cornell. Working with my advisor, Hunter Adams, I began investigating the possiblilities for comprehensive soil moisture monitoring for agriculture, specifically wine grapes. I began by investigating the possbility of an undeground sensor network but physics made commmunication between sensors immposible at the distances required. I have moved to an alternate plan of putting the sensors in "stakes" so that the communication equipment can have a much larger range. I am currently working on the protoype of this new idea.
